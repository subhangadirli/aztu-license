# Contributing to the AzTU License

Thank you for your interest in improving the AzTU License! Because a software license relies on stable text to remain legally coherent, our contribution process is strict.

#### What we accept right now:
* Typo fixes and grammar corrections.
* Clarifications in the `README.md`, `FAQ.md`, or `COMPARISON.md`.
* Translations of the license (hosted in a `/translations` directory, marked purely as informational).

#### What we do NOT accept right now:
* Pull requests altering the fundamental legal mechanics of the license. 
* Additions of new clauses without prior discussion.

If you have discovered a legal ambiguity or a loophole, please open an **Issue** first so we can discuss it publicly before drafting a change for v2.2.
