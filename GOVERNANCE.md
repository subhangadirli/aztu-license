# Governance Model

The AzTU License is maintained by its original authors. We operate under a **Maintainer Consensus** model. 

1. **Issues & Feedback:** Anyone can open an issue to point out flaws, ambiguities, or suggest improvements.
2. **Drafting:** Changes to the license text are drafted publicly in PRs.
3. **Approval:** A change to the actual `LICENSE.md` text requires explicit sign-off from the core maintainers and will always result in a version bump (e.g., v2.1 to v2.2) to protect existing users.

*Note: In the future, if this license achieves wide adoption, we intend to transition stewardship to a neutral foundation or legal trust.*
